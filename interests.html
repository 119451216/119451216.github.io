Sport

I have played hurling most of my life. I started playing the sport when I was 6 years old with my local club Newtownshandrum. Thoughout my juvenile years, I have achieved many things, including the honour of being captain of a winning Premier 2 U13 team and winning a Premier 1 title at U14. Subsequentually, my love for the sport has grown over the years. Currently I am still playing hurling with my club am I am playing Fresher B hurling with UCC. I am in my second season playing Premier Senior with my club in County Cork.

Sport
