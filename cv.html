Ditch Your Carbon Cup Print was an environmental student project designed to reduce the general population’s single-use disposable cup use. The project started as an entry to the Young Social Innovators Awards 2017. With our “Ditch Your Carbon Cup Print” campaign, I played a pivotal role as Team Leader and Marketing Manager in organising, pitching, presenting and leading our team. Through-out my involvement in this project, I have had the opportunity to make many useful and interesting contact with important and experienced people. I have also been able to learn new skills such as how to negociate, how to sell, how to pitch new project ideas and overall commuinicate in a professional manner with my peers and other business partners.   • YSI Make Your World Greener Challenge Winners 2017
